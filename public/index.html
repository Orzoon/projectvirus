Total cases of COVID-19

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that cause respiratory infections. These can range from the common cold to more serious diseases. COVID-19 is the disease caused by a new coronavirus. It was first reported in December 2019 in Wuhan City in China.

It is a new strain of coronaviruses that hasn’t previously been identified in humans.

There is no specific treatment for COVID-19 and, in most cases, symptoms will resolve on their own.There are no available vaccines for COVID-19 at this time.

COVID-19 Symptoms

Fever

In general, if you’re an adult and your temperature is above 100.4°F (38.0°C), you have a fever.
When somebody has a fever, signs and symptoms may include chills, dehydration, sore eyes, headache.

Flu-like Symptoms

Flu-like symptoms includes dry cough- can be dry or with phlegm, sore throat, runny nose, headaches and fatigue

Shortness of breath

More rarely and serious case, Older people, and people with other medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, or heart disease), may show this symptom.

COVID-19 prevention

Wash your hands

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub

Cover your nose and mouth

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze

Avoid close contact

Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell and avoid touching your face

Stay isolated

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell
